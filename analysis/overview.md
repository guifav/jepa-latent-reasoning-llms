# JEPA landscape overview

- Total corpus: **176** papers
- Core JEPA papers: **75**
- Adjacent papers: **101**
- Earliest paper in corpus: **2023-01-19**
- Most recent paper in corpus: **2026-04-22**

## Main takeaways

- The field starts in image-centric self-supervision, then expands quickly into motion/video, audio and multimodality.
- The steepest recent growth comes from world models, planning, JEPA-to-generation bridges, and theory/recipe papers.
- Language/reasoning is recent and still clearly early-stage compared with vision/video.
- Application growth is outpacing benchmarking discipline, which creates a comparison problem across papers.
