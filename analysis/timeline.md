## Phase view

- 2023 — foundations and first modality extensions: **3** papers
- 2024 — diversification into backbones, multimodality and conceptual bridges: **14** papers
- 2025 H1 — world models, policy learning and broader applications: **26** papers

## Milestones

- **2023-01-19** — `2301.08243` — **Self-Supervised Learning from Images with a Joint-Embedding Predictive Architecture**. Foundational image JEPA baseline; establishes the starting point for the whole literature.
- **2023-07-24** — `2307.12698` — **MC-JEPA: A Joint-Embedding Predictive Architecture for Self-Supervised Learning of Motion and Content Features**. First major temporal extension: JEPA for motion/content disentanglement in video.
- **2023-11-27** — `2311.15830` — **A-JEPA: Joint-Embedding Predictive Architecture Can Listen**. Early modality expansion from vision into audio.
- **2024-08-14** — `2408.07514` — **CNN-JEPA: Self-Supervised Pretraining Convolutional Neural Networks Using Joint Embedding Predictive Architecture**. Backbone diversification beyond ViT-heavy formulations into CNNs.
- **2024-09-09** — `2409.05929` — **M3-JEPA: Multimodal Alignment via Multi-gate MoE based on the Joint-Embedding Predictive Architecture**. Multimodal JEPA enters the literature in a dedicated form.
- **2024-10-02** — `2410.03755` — **Denoising with a Joint-Embedding Predictive Architecture**. Generative bridge appears via denoising-oriented JEPA framing.
- **2024-10-25** — `2410.19560` — **Connecting Joint-Embedding Predictive Architecture with Contrastive Self-supervised Learning**. The literature starts connecting JEPA to adjacent SSL theory such as contrastive learning.
- **2024-12-14** — `2412.10925` — **Video Representation Learning with Joint-Embedding Predictive Architectures**. JEPA becomes a broader video representation-learning family, not just a single paper line.
- **2025-01-24** — `2501.14622` — **ACT-JEPA: Novel Joint-Embedding Predictive Architecture for Efficient Policy Representation Learning**. JEPA enters policy representation learning and RL-oriented work.
- **2025-05-06** — `2505.03176` — **seq-JEPA: Autoregressive Predictive Learning of Invariant-Equivariant World Models**. Autoregressive / sequential world-model direction becomes explicit.
- **2025-06-11** — `2506.09985` — **V-JEPA 2: Self-Supervised Video Models Enable Understanding, Prediction and Planning**. V-JEPA 2 marks a step from representation learning toward understanding, prediction and planning.
- **2025-06-25** — `2507.02915` — **Audio-JEPA: Joint-Embedding Predictive Architecture for Audio Representation Learning**. Audio JEPA becomes a more explicit standalone program of work.
- **2025-09-11** — `2509.14252` — **LLM-JEPA: Large Language Models Meet Joint Embedding Predictive Architectures**. JEPA ideas move decisively into LLM territory.
- **2025-10-01** — `2510.00974` — **JEPA-T: Joint-Embedding Predictive Architecture with Text Fusion for Image Generation**. Image-generation bridge appears through JEPA + text fusion.
- **2025-11-11** — `2511.08544` — **LeJEPA: Provable and Scalable Self-Supervised Learning Without the Heuristics**. LeJEPA consolidates the theory/recipe turn with a cleaner and more principled training story.
- **2025-12-11** — `2512.10942` — **VL-JEPA: Joint Embedding Predictive Architecture for Vision-language**. Vision-language JEPA becomes explicit rather than implicit or ad hoc.
- **2025-12-22** — `2512.19171` — **JEPA-Reasoner: Decoupling Latent Reasoning from Token Generation**. Latent reasoning is framed as a first-class JEPA research direction.
- **2026-02-10** — `2602.10098` — **VLA-JEPA: Enhancing Vision-Language-Action Model with Latent World Model**. JEPA begins to plug directly into VLA systems.
- **2026-02-11** — `2602.11389` — **Causal-JEPA: Learning World Models through Object-Level Latent Interventions**. Object-centric causal/world-model JEPA becomes explicit and technically grounded.
- **2026-03-13** — `2603.19312` — **LeWorldModel: Stable End-to-End Joint-Embedding Predictive Architecture from Pixels**. End-to-end JEPA world models from pixels push toward unified latent world modeling.
- **2026-03-23** — `2603.22281` — **ThinkJEPA: Empowering Latent World Models with Large Vision-Language Reasoning Model**. JEPA starts being paired with stronger reasoning stacks for latent planning.
- **2026-04-22** — `2604.21046` — **JEPAMatch: Geometric Representation Shaping for Semi-Supervised Learning**. Recent work returns to representation shaping, indicating the objective-design frontier remains open.
